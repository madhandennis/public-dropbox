offense — gaps & upgrade opportunities
Throwing crit multi gap

Throwing sits at 548% vs melee 453%. Bladestorm and Shurikens are your primary damage dealers — stacking throwing crit multi further (Traitor's Tongue LP, rings) is your highest-leverage offensive upgrade.

Dagger count for Flow State

Flow State on Shadow Cascade gives +15% per dagger. You're dual-wielding two daggers — that's +30% more damage on Shadow Cascade already applied. Good synergy.

Leveraged Momentum undervalued

Only 3/3 allocated but gives +0.6% per 1% melee attack speed. With 70% increased melee speed, that's ~42% more damage on Shadow Cascade from this node alone. Good investment.

Dexterity is low (33)

Dex scales Dancing Strikes, Bladestorm, Shadow Cascade, and Shurikens at 4% damage per point. Velocyn's Jaw gives +3-4 to Dex skills and +10-16 Dex — at 33 Dex, you're leaving significant damage on the table. Pushing Dex to 50+ should be a gear priority.

defense — gaps & upgrade opportunities
Void resistance is the weak point

At 77% you are noticeably under the 75% soft cap but still exposed. Rings currently cover Necrotic and Poison — adding Void to one ring or upgrading Omnis rolls would close this. Priority: high.

Dodge is low for a Bladedancer

15% dodge chance from 458 rating is modest. Perfection + Confidence add armor/dodge per stack (+60 each at 10 stacks = +600 rating) making this much better in combat, but the base is thin. More Dex = more from Nimble on Dancing Strikes too.

Health could be higher

2,580 HP is on the lower end for endgame. Woven Flesh and the helmet contribute %, but the base health pool is fragile before Endurance kicks in. The idol Large Shadow slots have health % prefixes — upgrading those is easy health.

Full-health dependency risk

Confidence (+60% dmg at full HP), Asuvon's Pact (damage tripled at full HP), and All In create massive conditional bonuses — but you lose Perfection stacks on any hit. Smoke Bomb's Moonlight Bomb (Silver Shroud) and Cloaked Incursion help maintain this but it's a fragile playstyle at high corruption.
